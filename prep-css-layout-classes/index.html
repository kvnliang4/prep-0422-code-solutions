Indian Cajones: From Memery

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Our protagonist, Indiana Cajones, meets with the antagonist who is looking for the fabled lost ark, which is rumored to contain an item that allows one to talk to Jesus. After hearing the antagonist's plan, Cajones responds with "You want to talk to God? Let's go see him together...", and he chucks two live hand grenades into the center of the room. The antagonist dies from the explosion, but Cajones ends up surviving the blast because Spielberg needed him to enter the temple of doom later on.

Temple of Doom

While on the run, Cajones and his party stumbles upon a village and is asked to retrieve a rock and rescue some missing children. He agrees because shiny rock! and eventually finds his way to The Temple of Doom through a series of underground tunnels. In the temple, he spots a cult gathering, with the leader pulling a heart out of a person with his bare hands. Cajones decides he's not touching that with an 80-foot pole and instead calls for back up by sending messages to the US government, saying there is oil and weapons of mass destruction at his location. Within 48 hours, the US military arrives and overpowers the cult. The rock is returned and the children are saved.

The Last Crusade

After his dad went to buy some milk at the store, but never came back, Cajones tries to pursue the holy grail, which grants immortality. Coincidentally, the Nazis were after the cup as well. As a man from Indiana, Indiana could not just let that happen. He formed a party and began searching. After a few close encounters with death, the party eventually find thes the holy grail in a temple. However, a greedy wench tries to take it with her; the temple crumbles and the grail is lost.
